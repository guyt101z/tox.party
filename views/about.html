About


tox.party is a ToxDNS service written, hosted, and maintained, by a tech illiterate 14 year old.


Whilst I consider it to be stable, I may push changes that are not entirely tested, and there is a slight bug in the DNS server library I use (node-named), so try not to rely on my service too much.


The source is licensed under AGPLv3, and available at github.com/installgen2/tox.party.
